About Satisfactory Tools

Satisfactory Tools is a all-in-one planner for Satisfactory.

What's inside
  • Codex - browse items, buildings and milestones of any game version.
  • Planner - production plans with an optimising solver, manual graph editing, subplans and per-plan settings.
  • Custom versions - derive your own game version from a public one with gameplay modifiers (recipe cost, power cost) and mods baked into its data.
  • Mods - author sets of data changes (new or overridden items, recipes, buildings) and share them publicly or keep them private.
